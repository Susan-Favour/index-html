A Warm Welcome To AkiraChix

My First Week

My first week at AkiraChix has been a blissful experience and I have felt beyond welcomed ever since I entered these gates.To feel at home when not at home is such an underrated feeling and I am glad that I got the opportunity to be selected to be here.

Some of the good things I have experienced

  1. Good food
  2. Peaceful environment
  3. Diversified family
  4. Supportive Staff

Of course perfection does not exist so it would be a lie to say that some things haven't been well for me. However, I must note that it is very minimal and quite franky, beyond the institutions control. For instance;

  • The constant nagging of some girls
  • Unecessary noise at odd hours
  • The dragging of duties

In spite of these reasons, I am still motivated, receptive and optimistic of this new journey.

"Excellence Is Not An Option, It Is The Only Option"

This is the best quote I have heard at AkiraChix as its application is not just limited to my stay here, but also beyond.
By Susan Favour
